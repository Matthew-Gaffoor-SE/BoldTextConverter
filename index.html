Privacy Policy

BoldText Converter does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Text Processing

All text entered into the app is processed locally on the user’s device and is never transmitted or stored externally.

Advertising

BoldText Converter currently does not display advertisements.

If advertising is added in the future, the app may use third-party ad providers. These providers may collect limited device information in accordance with their own privacy policies.
